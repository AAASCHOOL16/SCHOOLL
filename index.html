About Us

As-Salam Anglo Arabic School was established in 2017 in the village of Salaiya, Post-Madhupur, Dist-Deoghar, Jharkhand, 815353. It is a primary school based on the CBSE curriculum with a focus on holistic education for all students.

The school was founded by Mr. Imamuddin, who is also the Chairman of Al-Imam Educational Trust.

Curriculum

Our school follows the CBSE curriculum. In addition to the standard subjects, we offer:

  • Urdu and Arabic for Muslim students.
  • Sanskrit for non-Muslim students.

Transportation

We provide safe and reliable transportation with our fleet of 3 school vans, ensuring students reach school and return home comfortably.
